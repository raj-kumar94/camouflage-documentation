# 2️ Step 2: Activate Camouflage in the theme

#### **After you've follwed the** [**step 1**](basic-configuration.md)**, you need to enable the app in one of your themes (if you want to test Camouflage first, you can enable it in a draft theme)**

Click on the "Enable Camouflage" button. It will redirect you to the theme customiser.  Click on the "Save" button at the top right corner to enable Camouflage in the theme customiser.

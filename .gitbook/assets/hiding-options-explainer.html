Camouflage · how hiding works

What actually happens to a variant, in each hiding option

Camouflage can hide a variant two fundamentally different ways. Knowing which one you're using tells you exactly where the variant disappears, and where it can still be found. Pick an option below and watch.

Conceal Hidden where Camouflage runs

The variant is hidden on your product pages, collection swatches, quick views and featured products, and blocked at checkout. It stays published, so it can still surface in search and other channels.

Unpublish Removed from the channel

The variant is actually unpublished from the sales channels, markets or catalogs you choose. It's gone from search, filters and collections there: the whole surface, not just where Camouflage runs.

Country hiding isn't location/IP based. "Hide from countries" hides for shoppers based on the market they're browsing or the country they pick in your store's country switcher, not their physical location.
Always show wins. An "Always show" override keeps a variant visible everywhere and beats every hide rule, including sold-out hiding.
Scheduled hiding is automatic. With a schedule, hiding switches on at your start time and off at your end time. With native publishing on, the variant is unpublished for the window and republished when it ends.
Plan availability. Native unpublishing and multiple channels start on paid plans; hiding from Markets and B2B catalogs is on Pro and above; hiding from countries is an Enterprise feature.
